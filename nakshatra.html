Nakshatra-phala Explanation

Auspicious Days Based on Area Calculation

Multiply the area by 9 and divide it by 7. If the remainder is 2, 4, 5, or 6, then the corresponding days are considered auspicious:

  • 2 - Monday
  • 4 - Wednesday
  • 5 - Thursday
  • 6 - Friday

Nakshatra Phala Explanation

The remainder of the field result multiplied by eight and divided by seven is the number of stars left. Among them are 12, 21, 26, 5, 4, 8, 17, 13, 14, 15, 23, 24, 27. The 13 stars corresponding to these numbers, Uttarashada, Uttarabhadra, Mrigashir, Rohini, Pushya, Anuradha, Hasta, Chitta, Swati, Danishta, Shatatara, Revati, give very auspicious results. The remaining stars are auspicious.
