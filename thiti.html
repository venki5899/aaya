Tithi Phala Explanation

  • Multiply the inner-to-inner dimension of the building by 8.
  • Divide the result by 30.
  • The remainder is considered the Tithi (lunar day).
  • The following Tithis are considered auspicious:
    • 2
    • 3
    • 5
    • 6
    • 7
    • 10
    • 11
  • Full Moon (15) and New Moon (30) are also considered auspicious.
  • All other Tithis are considered inauspicious.
